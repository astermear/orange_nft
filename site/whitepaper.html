Whitepaper

Author: AsterMear

1. Introduction
Ornage is a unique NFT collection that represents a fusion of traditional art with blockchain technologies. Deployed on the Optimism network, Ornage utilizes the NAP (NFT Automated Market Protocol) technology, based on the ERC404 standard, to create a flexible and decentralized platform for trading and investing in digital assets.

2. Project Goals
- Creating an innovative NFT collection that merges traditional art with blockchain technologies.
- Integrating decentralized finance (DeFi) mechanisms into the NFT segment through the use of the NAP protocol.
- Supporting creative communities and artists by providing opportunities to create and monetize their unique artworks.

3. Ornage NFT Collection Features
- Innovative NAP Technology: Utilizing the NAP protocol, Ornage allows trading NFTs like regular tokens, opening up new possibilities for investment and trading.
- Decentralized Platform: Ornage operates on the Optimism blockchain, providing high transaction speeds and low fees.
- Unique Artworks: Each NFT in the Ornage collection is unique and created by talented artists, who are rewarded for their creative efforts.

4. NFT Creation and Trading Process
- NFT Creation: Artists can upload their artworks to the Ornage platform, creating unique NFTs using the NAP protocol.
- NFT Trading: Users can buy and sell NFTs on the Ornage platform, as well as use them for investment or collecting purposes.

5. Economic Model
- Transaction Fees: A portion of the fees from NFT trading on the Ornage platform is redistributed to artists as rewards for creating content.
- Mining Participation: Ornage NFT holders can participate in the mining process using the NAP protocol, receiving rewards in project tokens.

6. Future Plans and Project Development
- Collection Expansion: Ornage plans to regularly add new unique artworks to the collection.
- Integration with Other Platforms: We aim to establish partnerships with other platforms and services to expand the accessibility and convenience of Ornage.

7. Conclusion
The Ornage NFT Collection is an innovative project that bridges the worlds of art and blockchain technologies. We believe that our collection will become a valuable asset for collectors and investors, as well as help support and develop creative communities worldwide.
